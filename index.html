About

Content operations, handled.

I'm Ella Joy — I run social media and YouTube content behind the scenes for creators and small teams who don't have time to plan, edit, and post every day.

Right now I manage the Shorts pipeline for a software engineering YouTube channel, turning long-form ideas into short, watchable clips, plus building carousels for social platforms that break down complex topics into simple, swipeable posts.

My focus is consistency: a steady content calendar, on-brand editing, and posting that actually keeps a channel growing — not a one-off video that disappears.

Fun fact: before content, I worked as a collections analyst. Different world entirely — but it's where I learned to stay organized and get things done on deadline. Onsite just for good times these days. 😄
